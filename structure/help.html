Help & Support

Find answers to frequently asked questions or contact support

How to create profile?

Login → Complete your profile details → Upload your resume → Save

How to apply for a drive?

Go to Placement Drives → View details → Click Apply

How to check eligibility?

Go to Check Eligibility section from homepage Quick Actions.

How to download offer letter?

Go to Download Offer in Quick Actions if you are selected.
